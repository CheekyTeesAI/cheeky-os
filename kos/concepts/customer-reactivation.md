# Customer Reactivation
Tags: #concept #retention #revenue
Source references: [Automation Jobs](../wiki/automation-jobs.md)

## Definition
Customer reactivation identifies lapsed customers and produces targeted outreach to recover dormant revenue.

## Key principles
- Segment by inactivity duration.
- Prioritize by lifetime spend.
- Generate actionable messaging artifacts before channel execution.
