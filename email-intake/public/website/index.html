What We Do

Full-service custom apparel from a real shop with real people.

Screen Printing

The classic. Vibrant, durable ink on any garment. Best for bulk orders with consistent designs.

DTG (Direct to Garment)

Full-color photo-quality prints with no minimums. Perfect for complex artwork or small runs.

DTF (Direct to Film)

Transfer prints that work on virtually any fabric. Sharp edges, soft hand-feel, long-lasting color.

Embroidery

Stitched logos and text for a premium, professional look. Ideal for polos, jackets, hats, and uniforms.

Team Uniforms

Outfitting your whole team? We handle school districts, sports teams, and corporate programs at scale.

Event Apparel

Race shirts, fundraiser tees, family reunion gear — we love a good event. Fast turnaround, no drama.

Private Online Stores

We run private, managed stores for schools, teams, and companies. Employees or members order directly — no purchase orders, no spreadsheets, no guesswork.
